About Us

Bias Buster News is a project born out of the passion for promoting informed and open-minded citizenship. Developed by four first-year University of Washington students during the 2023 DubHacks hackathon, our mission is to break down informational barriers and broaden horizons. In a world where echo chambers and polarized opinions can dominate the news landscape, we aim to provide a fresh perspective. Bias Buster News introduces you to news articles and topics that you might not typically explore, fostering a deeper understanding of political landscapes and diverse viewpoints. We believe that well-informed citizens are essential for a thriving democracy, and we're committed to helping you become one. Begin your journey by sliding through the different political views to personalize the news articles recommended to you.
